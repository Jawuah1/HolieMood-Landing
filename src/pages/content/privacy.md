### Health and wellness data

HolieMood includes features that can process information about Your mood, emotions, and wellbeing. Depending on how You use the Application, the Application may **access, collect, store, or process** the following categories of **health and wellness data** (in addition to Personal Data and Usage Data described above). **How** that happens—and whether We can read it on Our servers—differs by feature; see **Encryption and what We can read** below.

| Category | Examples of what this may include | How it is used |
| -------- | --------------------------------- | -------------- |
| Mood and emotional state | Mood selections, labels, ratings, or similar inputs You provide for tracking or emotional scripture matching (including optional short notes if a feature lets You add them) | To run mood tracking, match verses and content to the state You indicate, and show You history inside the app where the feature provides it |
| Journal entries | Private journal content You write in the journal section | Encrypted so that the **Company cannot read** the plaintext of Your entries; used only so You can view and manage Your journal on Your devices |
| Favorited AI responses | AI replies You choose to save as favorites | Stored **encrypted** so that the **Company cannot read** the plaintext; used only so You can keep and reopen those favorites in the app |
| Other saved study content | For example favorite verses or similar items You save outside the journal or favorited-AI flows | To store and display those saves in the Application as You expect |
| Guided breathwork / mindfulness-style activity | Session or feature usage tied to breathwork (e.g., that You started or completed a session), preferences You set in the app | To deliver guided breathwork, remember Your preferences, and improve those features |
| AI companion session messages | Things You type in the AI chat during an active session, which may mention feelings, stress, faith questions, or personal situations | **Not stored in Our databases.** Messages exist **only on Your device** for the **active session** and are **removed when the session ends**; We **do not** keep chat history on Our servers after that. A prompt may be sent to an AI **Service Provider** **only** to generate a reply for that request; We do **not** retain that conversation in Our systems afterward |

We use health and wellness data **only** to provide, maintain, secure, and improve the Application and its features, to communicate with You about the Service where appropriate, and to meet legal obligations. We **do not** sell health or wellness data. We **do not** use this data to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment; HolieMood is **not** a substitute for professional medical or mental health care.

**Encryption and what We can read:** Journal entries and favorited AI responses are protected with **encryption** so that **We cannot access or read** their contents on Our end—they are for **Your** use inside the app only. **AI chat messages are not saved to Our databases**; they live on Your device for the session and then are gone from a storage perspective on Our side as described above. Other items (such as mood selections, account data, usage or diagnostic data, or ciphertext blobs We cannot decrypt) may still be processed or hosted as needed to run the Service.

**Third parties and processing:** We use **Service Providers** (for example cloud hosting, databases, analytics, crash reporting, authentication, payment processors, and—**during an active AI request**—model providers) where needed to operate the Application. **We do not provide those providers with readable journal or favorited-AI content** in plaintext. For AI replies, **only what is necessary to fulfill the current request** may be processed by the model provider for that moment, consistent with the session behavior above. Other processing follows Our agreements and applicable law.

**Platform health services:** The Application **does not** read from or write to Apple Health, Google Health Connect, or other similar platform-level health or fitness databases. Health and wellness data is limited to what You provide or generate inside HolieMood as described in the table above.
